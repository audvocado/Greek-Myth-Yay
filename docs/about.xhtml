Project Details

Methodology

Our research utilizes XML markup and computational text analysis to track how specific language within the translations affects Medea as a character and her experience in motherhood. By tagging differences in details, we can visualize the contrast between a character's agency and the translators interpretation.

Research Questions

  • How do the translators background and historical context affect their translation?
  • How does the Medea as a character change depending on each text?
  • How do the theme topics of motherhood and power differ between each interpretation?

Project Goals

Ultimately, this project seeks to challenge traditional readings of Greek literature by highlighting the complex, often contradictory ways power was granted to, and stripped away from, mothers in power facing male dominated worlds.
