Motherhood and Power: Medea Through the Translations

Our Mission

With this project, we want to discover how Medea as a mother and an authority figure has morphed and been reinterpreted throughout various translations. By looking at different texts featuring the same passage, we can discover how slight changes within each translation affects her overall character and her experiences through motherhood. For our analysis, we'll be looking at four diffferent translations of a Medea passage completed by authors of demographic, time, and language diversity. We'll use the same schema across all four documents to promote comparison and detailed argument.
