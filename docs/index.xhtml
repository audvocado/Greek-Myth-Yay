Women in Power

Our Mission [−]

With this project, we want to discover how women in power in Greek texts have been depicted through various lenses. We aim to look at themes of motherhood and percieved deception/witchcraft, descriptive language and adjectives, and dialougue to see how the women are percieved from the male perspective.
