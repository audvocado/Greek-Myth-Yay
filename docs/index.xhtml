Women in Power

Exploring the Depiction of Female Figures in Greek Myth

Mission

With this project, we want to discover how women in power in Greek texts have been depicted through various lenses. We aim to look at themes of motherhood and perceived deception/witchcraft, descriptive language and adjectives, and dialogue to see how the women are perceived from the male perspective.
